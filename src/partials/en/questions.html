F.A.Q.

  • How much does the app cost?
    Downloading the app is free for all users. No user fee is charged from the sender. The carrier pays 10% of the order value + VAT after successful delivery. For example, you transported goods and earned 100 euros. After successful delivery, you will receive an invoice in the application. Its amount will be 10 euros + VAT.
  • Can I skip verification?
    Verification is required for all users. Choose a convenient method - using a credit card or PayPal. For verification, the system will withdraw 0.50 euros from the account, and you will receive a code that you need to enter. Users with a verified PayPal account can verify their identity for free and in a few clicks.
  • Who sees my data?
    We respect privacy rules, users can see the following data: the name of the sender's driver, photo and ratings. You exchange personal information (email, phone number) only with the user you make a deal with. Ship&Deal does not transfer personal data to third parties. For more information, please see our privacy policy.
  • Does Ship&Deal insure goods?
    Over many years of cooperation with forwarders and sending insured goods (including oversized ones), we have come to the conclusion that insurance does not give any privileges. We have never been compensated for the damage, they always found reasons why it was impossible to do this. Therefore, at this stage, we do not insure goods, but we are working on solving this issue so that everything goes smoothly and economically
