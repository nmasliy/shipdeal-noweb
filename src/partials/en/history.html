History of Ship & Deal

Try Ship & Deal and make sure that goods delivery is not a problem, but a simple task that the best performers can handle

The history of Ship&Deal began with the company AUTODOGS, which deals with…
We used the services of various transport companies for the delivery of mostly oversized cargo, but we constantly faced problems:

  • High prices
  • Long delivery times
  • Damage to goods during transportation (despite the
    payment of insurance, the damage was not reimbursed)
  • Delivery to the wrong address (resulting in additional shipping
    costs to redirect the shipment to the correct address)
  • Overpayment up to 20% of the cost of the goods for packaging

Because of this, the dissatisfaction of AUTODOGS customers grew. We
considered this an injustice and have been looking for a solution for many years. But they just ran into the same problems.
Then an idea came: if you can’t win, lead! This is how the Ship&Deal project was born.
We took into account all the negative experience gained over the years of cooperation with forwarders and implemented simple solutions in one
application.
